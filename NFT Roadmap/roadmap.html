NFT COLLECTION ROADMAP!

!!ROADMAP!!

!!STAGE 1!!

-The NFT Collection Roadmap will be activated after the 10k collection is sold out.

-They will be given access to vote upon which community games will be played by the project's community.

!!STAGE 2!!

-The original holders of the 25 Bull NFT'S will recieve NFT airdrops.

-These airdrops will grant them the access of having their own exclusive floor in the communities Skyscrapper in the metaverse.

-Holders of the 50 Bull NFT'S will recieve 10 utility tokens every week, which will grant them the access of buying NFT'S to customise their skyscrapper floors.

-That also includes the projects community

-NFT holders will be able to burn their NFT'S when they have 450 utility tokens.

-When members have 580 utility tokens they will then have the option of breeding their NFT'S with the next GEN Bull.

-An online community tournament will be held where the winner of the tournament will win 750 utility tokens.

-Community members will decide the tournament format and the game to be played in the community tournament.
